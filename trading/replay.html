How to watch this

Blue is buy & hold; olive is the trend+regime strategy; the strip under the chart shows the three signals each month (top row trend, middle credit, bottom vol regime — red means warning). Watch 2008: the signals stack, the strategy steps aside, and the blue line falls twice as far as the olive one. Then watch 2010–2019: repeated false alarms where de-risking cost a little upside each time. That's the honest shape of the trade — insurance premiums are paid in good years, the payout comes in the terrible ones. The tally cards keep the running score so you can judge whether the insurance was worth it at any point in history.
